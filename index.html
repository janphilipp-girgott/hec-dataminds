Our Activities

Coding Sessions

We meet every Wednesday at 17:00 in S125 to code! We learn new skills on Datacamp or work jointly on coding projects.

Networking Events

We organize networking events for students to find internships for their gap years. We also host pre-POWs on Thursdays, so feel free to join!

Data Consulting for Good

Teams of up to four students advise startups and social organizations on data science topics as pro-bono consultants.

Company Presentations

Regularly, we invite companies for presentations. They help us in our coding projects and give crucial advice for consulting work.
